## 1. Your Consumer Rights

ReadingDaily Scripture App respects your consumer rights under national and international consumer protection laws. This guide explains your rights when using our App and subscribing to our services.

## 2. Purchase and Subscription Rights

### 2.1 Right to Information

You have the right to:
- Clear, accurate information about the App before purchase
- Transparent pricing in your local currency
- Information about subscription auto-renewal terms
- Notification of price changes before renewal
- Clear cancellation instructions

### 2.2 Right to Cancel

You have the right to cancel your subscription:
- At any time without penalty
- Up to 14 days after purchase for a full refund (where applicable by law)
- Your cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period
- Cancellation can be completed through:
  - App settings (Settings > Subscription > Cancel)
  - Your device app store (Apple App Store, Google Play Store)
  - Contacting ourenglish2019@gmail.com

### 2.3 Right to Refund

You may be entitled to a refund:
- Within 14 days of purchase (EU/UK residents)
- Within 30 days of purchase (some US states)
- For unauthorized charges
- For services not provided as described

**Refund Process:**
1. Contact ourenglish2019@gmail.com with your order details
2. Provide reason for refund request
3. We will investigate and process within 14 days
4. Refunds are issued to original payment method

**Note:** Refund eligibility varies by jurisdiction and payment method.

### 2.4 Free Trial Terms

If a free trial is offered:
- Trial terms are clearly disclosed before signup
- Subscription automatically begins unless cancelled before trial ends
- You can cancel during the free trial with no charges
- Trial period is extended to account for your time zone

- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your data (right to be forgotten)

**Under CCPA (California residents):**
- Know what personal information is collected
- Delete your personal information
- Opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)
- Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

**Under other jurisdictions:**
- Similar rights may apply in your location
- Contact ourenglish2019@gmail.com for jurisdiction-specific rights

### 4.3 Your Data and Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at:

## 7. Consumer Protection Laws by Region

### 7.1 European Union (GDPR & Consumer Rights Directive)

**You have the right to:**
- 14-day right of withdrawal for distance purchases
- Mandatory pre-contract information
- Clear cancellation procedures
- Data portability
- Right to be forgotten
- Protection against unfair contract terms

**Consumer Authority Contact:**
- Find your national consumer protection authority at:
  https://ec.europa.eu/info/about-european-commission/contacts_en

### 7.2 United Kingdom (GDPR & Consumer Rights Act 2015)

**You have the right to:**
- 14-day cooling-off period
- Goods/services conform to contract
- Supplied with reasonable care and skill
- Cancellation without penalty
- Data subject rights under UK GDPR

**Regulator:** Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)
https://ico.org.uk

### 7.3 United States

**By State:**

**California (CCPA & Consumer Legal Remedies Act):**
- Right to know, delete, opt-out, and non-discrimination
- Attorney General can enforce consumer protection laws
- Contact: https://caag.ca.gov

**New York (General Business Law):**
- Deceptive practices prohibited
- Right to accurate disclosure
- Contact: ny.ag.gov

**Other States:**
- Similar consumer protection laws apply
- State Attorneys General enforce consumer protection

### 7.4 Canada

**Under PIPEDA and Relevant Provincial Laws:**
- Right to access personal information
- Right to correct inaccurate data
- Right to challenge collection, use, or disclosure
- Right to file complaint with Privacy Commissioner

**Privacy Commissioner of Canada:** https://www.priv.gc.ca

### 7.5 Australia

**Under Australian Consumer Law and Privacy Act:**
- Goods/services must be safe, durable, fit for purpose
- Protection against misleading/deceptive conduct
- Right to access and correct personal information

**Regulator:** Australian Competition and Consumer Commission (ACCC)
https://www.accc.gov.au
