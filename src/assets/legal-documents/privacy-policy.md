## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

### 4.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share data with trusted service providers who assist us:

- **Cloud Services:** Firebase (Google) - stores reading history, progress, settings
- **Payment Processing:** Stripe, RevenueCat - processes subscriptions (card info not shared)
- **Analytics:** Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel - analyzes app usage (anonymized)
- **Audio Processing:** Google Cloud Speech-to-Text (for pronunciation assessment)
- **Authentication:** Firebase Auth - manages login and security

These providers are contractually obligated to use your data only for the specified purposes.

### 4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Enforce our agreements
- Protect the safety and rights of users, the public, or our company

### 4.4 Business Transfers

If our company is acquired, merged, or assets are sold, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will provide notice before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services:

- **Account Data:** Retained while account is active, deleted upon request
- **Reading History:** Retained for 2 years or until deleted by user
- **Audio Recordings:** Stored temporarily for processing, then deleted
- **Analytics Data:** Retained for 12 months (anonymized after 6 months)
- **Backup Data:** Cloud backups retained according to Firebase retention policies

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted using TLS 1.2+
- **Encryption at Rest:** Sensitive data encrypted in Firestore database
- **Authentication:** Firebase authentication with secure password hashing
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access to sensitive information
- **Regular Audits:** Security reviews and vulnerability assessments

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.
