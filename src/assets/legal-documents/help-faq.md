## Getting Started

## Features and Usage

### How do I view today's reading?

**Automatic:**
- The app displays today's reading on the home screen by default

**Manual:**
1. Tap "Readings" tab
2. Current date is highlighted
3. Tap date to view reading
4. Reading details open automatically

### How do I access readings from other dates?

1. Tap "Readings" tab
2. Use the calendar or date picker
3. Select desired date
4. Tap "View Reading"

**Offline Readings:**
If you've downloaded readings for offline use, they're available even without internet.

### How do I download readings for offline use?

**Individual Readings:**
1. Open a reading
2. Tap "Download" (download icon)
3. Wait for download to complete

**Bulk Downloads:**
1. Tap "Readings" tab
2. Tap "Download Multiple" (or "Offline" option)
3. Select date range
4. Tap "Download All"
5. Check storage space (estimated MB shown)

**Storage Limit:**
Your app has a 50 MB storage limit for offline content. Older readings may be deleted automatically to make room.

### How do I use the pronunciation practice feature?

1. Tap "Practice" tab
2. Select today's date or choose a different date
3. View practice sentences for the day
4. Tap a sentence to practice
5. Tap the microphone icon
6. Read aloud when prompted
7. Your pronunciation is assessed in real-time
8. Review feedback and tips

**Premium feature:** Full assessment requires premium subscription.

### What languages does the translation feature support?

**Supported Languages:**
- Spanish (Español)
- French (Français)
- German (Deutsch)
- Portuguese (Português)
- Italian (Italiano)
- And more...

**To Translate:**
1. Open any reading
2. Tap the "Translate" icon
3. Select desired language
4. Translation appears inline or in sidebar
5. Tap back to see original

### How do I track my reading progress?

1. Tap "Progress" tab
2. View reading calendar
3. Green checkmarks show days you read
4. Statistics show:
   - Days read this month
   - Consistency percentage
   - Current streak
   - Historical trends

### How do I enable notifications?

1. Open Settings
2. Tap "Notifications"
3. Enable notification types:
   - Daily reminders
   - Achievement announcements
   - Subscription reminders
   - System updates
4. Set preferred time for daily reminder
5. Tap "Save"

**Note:** You must grant app permissions in device settings for notifications to work.

## Troubleshooting

### The app won't load. What should I do?

**Step 1: Basic Restart**
1. Force close the app (swipe up from bottom on iOS, long-press app on Android)
2. Wait 10 seconds
3. Reopen the app

**Step 2: Check Internet**
1. Ensure you have internet connection (WiFi or mobile)
2. Try another app to confirm connectivity
3. Reconnect to network if needed

**Step 3: Clear Cache**
1. Open Settings
2. Tap "Storage & Cache"
3. Tap "Clear Cache"
4. Reopen app

**Step 4: Update App**
1. Open App Store or Google Play
2. Search "ReadingDaily Scripture App"
3. If "Update" button appears, tap it
4. Wait for update to complete
5. Reopen app

**Still Not Working?**
Contact ourenglish2019@gmail.com with:
- Device type and OS version
- App version (in Settings)
- Error message (if any)
- Steps you've tried

### The app crashes when I open it. How do I fix this?

**Quick Fixes:**
1. Restart your device
2. Update to latest app version
3. Free up device storage space (delete unnecessary files)
4. Reinstall the app (back up your data first)

**Before Reinstalling:**
1. Go to Settings > Account
2. Download your reading history (if available)
3. Note your subscription status
4. Screenshot important information

**To Reinstall:**
1. Delete the app from your device
2. Restart device
3. Reinstall from App Store/Play Store
4. Log in with your account

**Still Crashing?**
Email ourenglish2019@gmail.com with crash logs and device details.

### I can't log in. What should I do?

**Check:**
1. Verify email address (no typos)
2. Confirm password is correct (check Caps Lock)
3. Ensure you're using the same email you signed up with

**If Forgotten Password:**
1. Tap "Forgot Password?" on login screen
2. Enter your email
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create new password
5. Log in with new password

**Email Not Found:**
1. Try alternative emails if you have multiple
2. You may need to sign up as a new account
3. Contact ourenglish2019@gmail.com for account recovery

### The reading content isn't displaying properly. How do I fix this?

**Try:**
1. Refresh the screen (pull down to refresh)
2. Switch between light and dark mode (Settings > Theme)
3. Adjust font size (Settings > Display > Font Size)
4. Close and reopen the app
5. Update to latest app version

**If Text is Unreadable:**
1. Go to Settings > Display
2. Increase Font Size
3. Enable "Larger Accessibility Sizes" (device settings)
4. Enable High Contrast (Settings > Display)

### Audio playback isn't working. What should I do?

**Check:**
1. Device volume is turned up (not muted)
2. Audio isn't disabled (Settings > Audio)
3. Audio file has downloaded completely (check download status)
4. Try a different audio to narrow down issue

**Fix Audio:**
1. Settings > Audio > Reset Audio Settings
2. Close and reopen app
3. Restart device
4. Check device speaker/headphone functionality

### The offline readings won't download. How do I troubleshoot?

**Check:**
1. You have internet connection (WiFi recommended for large downloads)
2. Sufficient storage space available (check device storage)
3. Reading is available for your date range
4. App has necessary permissions (Settings > Permissions)

**Try:**
1. Download a single reading first (not bulk)
2. Use WiFi instead of cellular
3. Free up device storage (delete unused apps)
4. Force close and reopen app
5. Update app to latest version

**Still Not Working?**
Contact support with:
- Dates you're trying to download
- Available storage space (from device settings)
- Error message (if any)

### How do I free up storage space?

**In-App Storage Management:**
1. Settings > Storage
2. View storage breakdown
3. Delete old readings (Settings > Storage > Manage Downloads)
4. Clear cache (Settings > Storage > Clear Cache)

**Device Storage:**
1. Delete unused apps
2. Clear app caches (device settings)
3. Delete old photos/videos
4. Free up space and retry download

## Accessibility and Accessibility Features

### Is the app accessible for people with disabilities?

Yes! The app includes:
- Screen reader support (VoiceOver on iOS, TalkBack on Android)
- Adjustable font sizes and text options
- High contrast mode
- Dark mode
- Large touch targets
- Keyboard navigation

### How do I enable screen reader support?

**iOS (VoiceOver):**
1. Device Settings > Accessibility > VoiceOver
2. Turn VoiceOver ON
3. Open ReadingDaily app
4. VoiceOver will read all text and buttons

**Android (TalkBack):**
1. Device Settings > Accessibility > TalkBack
2. Turn TalkBack ON
3. Open ReadingDaily app
4. TalkBack will read all text and buttons

### How do I increase font size?

1. Open Settings
2. Tap "Display"
3. Adjust Font Size slider
4. Choose size: Small, Medium, Large, Extra Large

**System-Wide Font:**
You can also increase system font in device settings for even larger text across all apps.

### How do I enable dark mode?

1. Settings > Theme
2. Choose: Light, Dark, or System Default
3. Dark mode activates immediately

### I'm having accessibility issues. How can I report them?

Contact us:
- Email: ourenglish2019@gmail.com
- In-App: Settings > Legal & Compliance > Report Accessibility Issue
- Include:
  - Accessibility tool you're using (VoiceOver, TalkBack, etc.)
  - Description of the barrier
  - Device type and OS version
  - Steps to reproduce

We respond within 24 hours.
