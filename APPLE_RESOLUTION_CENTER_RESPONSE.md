### **Response to Apple App Review Team**

Copy and paste this into the Resolution Center when submitting Build 70:

Hello App Review Team,

Thank you for your feedback regarding our app submission.

**Regarding Guideline 5.1.1 (Registration Before IAP):**

We understand the concern about requiring account creation before in-app purchases. Our subscription is inherently account-based and requires an account for the following technical reasons:

1. **Cross-Device Synchronization**: The subscription enables users to sync their reading progress, bookmarks, pronunciation practice recordings, notes, and settings across all their devices (iPhone, iPad). This cloud synchronization requires persistent user storage tied to an authenticated account.

2. **Cloud Backup and Restoration**: Users can restore all their data when switching devices or reinstalling the app by logging into their existing account, ensuring continuity of their spiritual practice.

3. **Subscription Restoration Across Devices**: When users get a new device, they can restore their active subscription by logging into their existing account, complementing Apple's standard subscription restoration mechanism.

4. **Free Access Without Account**: Users can explore the app and access daily Scripture readings during the trial period without creating an account. Account creation is only required when they choose to purchase the subscription to enable the cloud synchronization features.

We have updated our app screens to clearly communicate that account creation is specifically for enabling cross-device synchronization and cloud backup of user data. The account is not a barrier to purchase, but rather a necessary component of the subscription's core functionality.

We believe this falls within the acceptable use cases outlined in Guideline 5.1.1, as the subscription's primary value proposition (cross-device sync of religious practice data) inherently requires cloud storage tied to a user account.

**Regarding Guideline 2.1.0 (In-App Purchase Submission):**

We have now created and submitted all three in-app purchase products for review with the required screenshots:
- Monthly Premium Subscription (`com.readingdaily.basic.monthly`)
- Yearly Premium Subscription (`com.readingdaily.basic.yearly`)
- Lifetime Access (`com.readingdaily.lifetime.access`)

All IAP products are marked as "Ready to Submit" and will be submitted alongside this Build 70 binary for review.

**Additional Improvements in Build 70:**
- Updated app icon with professional design
- Fixed pronunciation practice recording functionality
- Improved dark mode text contrast
- Updated messaging to clarify account benefits

We appreciate your time reviewing our app and look forward to providing Catholic daily Scripture readings to our users.

Best regards,
ReadingDaily Scripture Development Team
