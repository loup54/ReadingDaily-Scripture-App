## Task 1: Complete Ads Declaration

### Purpose
Tell Google if your app displays advertisements to users.

### Answer: NO

ReadingDaily Scripture does **NOT** show ads. It's a premium app with subscriptions.

### How to Complete

1. **Go to Google Play Console:**
   - https://play.google.com/console
   - Select "ReadingDaily Scripture"

2. **Navigate to App Content:**
   - Left sidebar → **"App content"**
   - Find **"Ads declaration"** section
   - Click on it

3. **Answer the Questions:**

   **Question: "Does your app contain ads?"**
   - Select: **NO** ❌

   **Why:**
   - ReadingDaily is a paid subscription app
   - No third-party advertisements
   - No ad networks integrated (Google AdMob, Facebook Audience Network, etc.)
   - Users pay for ad-free experience

4. **Save:**
   - Click "Save" at the bottom
   - Return to Publishing overview

### Expected Result
✅ "Ads declaration" marked as complete
✅ Store listing will show "No ads" badge

## Task 2: Set App Category

### Purpose
Help users find your app in the correct category on Google Play Store.

### Answer: Education

ReadingDaily Scripture is an **educational app** teaching:
- Scripture reading
- Pronunciation practice
- Religious education
- Language learning (English through scripture)

### How to Complete

1. **Go to Google Play Console:**
   - https://play.google.com/console
   - Select "ReadingDaily Scripture"

2. **Navigate to Store Settings:**
   - Left sidebar → **"Store settings"**
   - Find **"App category"** section
   - Click "Select category" or "Edit"

3. **Choose Category:**
   - Primary category: **Education**
   - Sub-category (if applicable): **Educational**

   **Why Education (not Books & Reference):**
   - App teaches pronunciation (educational feature)
   - AI-powered assessment (learning tool)
   - Progress tracking (educational metric)
   - Not just passive reading

   **Alternative if Education doesn't fit:**
   - Books & Reference
   - Lifestyle (if religious focus emphasized)

4. **Add Tags (Optional):**
   - Scripture
   - Bible
   - Religious
   - Education
   - Learning

5. **Save:**
   - Click "Save" at the bottom
   - Return to Store settings

### Expected Result
✅ "App category" marked as complete
✅ App appears in Education category on Google Play
✅ Better discoverability for target audience
